A bunch of text files containing info datamined from Stardew Valley 1.4 that I was interested in. It's not terribly pretty or well organized, and some information is just my "best guess" at how to interpret something.

  • All tailoring recipes
  • All hats and how to get them
  • All Shirts and how to get them
  • Items for each dye color, including everything that counts for each of the pot colors
  • Fishpond data, including production chances
  • Villager movie preferences and special reactions
  • Villager movie concession preferences
  • Various special item tags and which items use them
  • Events which are not explicitly skippable
