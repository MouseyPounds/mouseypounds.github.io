A bunch of text files containing info datamined from Stardew Valley 1.4 that I was interested in. It's not terribly pretty or well organized, and some information is just my "best guess" at how to interpret something. Also, it's spoilers. Massive spoilers.

  • Tailoring - All tailoring recipes
  • Hats - All hats and how to get them
  • Shirts - All Shirts and how to get them
  • Dyes - Items for each dye color, including everything that counts for each of the pot colors
  • Ponds - Fishpond data, including production chances
  • Movies - Villager movie preferences and special reactions
  • Snacks - Villager movie concession preferences
  • Tags - Various special item tags and which items use them
  • Events - Events which are not explicitly skippable
