Input

There are two different ways to generate the item list:

  1. Wardrobe Input where you copy & paste the source of your Wardrobe page. This method will only pick from items you actually have and will try to avoid slot-overloading, but there isn't currently any way to filter the choices. It also includes thumbnails of the picked items since that is part of the wardrobe information.
  2. Filtered Input where the generator picks from a filtered list of all known Dappervolk items. This method may select items you don't actually have and does not take wardrobe slots into consideration so could generate impossible combinations. Filter choices are saved with cookies (if allowed) so that they persist over multiple visits to the site.
