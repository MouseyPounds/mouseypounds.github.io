Dappervolk Outfit Prompt Generator

Picks some random DV items to incorporate into an outfit. Feel free to DM me on DV (MouseyPounds #23596) or Discord (MouseyPounds#0557) for suggestions, bug reports, and other feedback.

Most recent changes (22 December 2022):

  • People seem to really like the new method where you supply what items you have by pasting the wardrobe source and the randomizer only picks from them. And since keeping the item list updated was very tedious (and I had fallen six months behind on it) I have decided to drop the old method of filtering from all known items entirely.
  • Output now lists what wardrobe slot the items chosen are found in.

Input

How to use:

  1. Go to your wardrobe page on Dappervolk.
  2. View the source of the wardrobe page and copy everything.
  3. Paste what you copied into the box below.
  4. Choose how many items you want the randomizer to grab.
  5. Hit the "Generate Items from Wardrobe" Button.
